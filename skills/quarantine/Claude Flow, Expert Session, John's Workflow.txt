PART 3: THE ART OF WORKFLOW (Philosophy)
Principle 1: Commands Are Contracts
A command is a promise: "When I say X, do Y."
Example - Bad Command:
You: "Build me a trading bot"
AI: *tries to do everything at once, fails*

Example - Good Command:
You: "/plan-trading-bot SOL-USDC arbitrage with 2% threshold"

Command triggers:
1. Researcher Agent → Study SOL-USDC liquidity patterns
2. Architect Agent → Design the bot architecture  
3. Store findings in Supabase vectors
4. Generate planning document
5. WAIT for your approval

The command doesn't DO the work. It ORCHESTRATES the work.
